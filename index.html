User Flow Story (Detailed)

1. Platform Owner (Super Admin)

Super Admin is the owner of UpBuild SaaS. Logs in securely, creates and manages new apartment complexes (tenants), monitors platform-wide activities, defines platform-level settings, assigns platform staff, and has full control.

2. Platform Admin

Platform Admins assist the Super Admin. They help onboard apartment complexes, configure settings, support tenant admins, and monitor usage. They have access to all tenant activities but limited platform setting access.

3. Tenant Creation (Apartment Complex)

Super Admin or Platform Admin creates a new Tenant profile: name, address, units, contact details, visitor and maintenance settings. A unique tenantId is generated internally to segregate tenant-specific data.

4. Assigning Apartment Admin

Super Admin assigns an Apartment Admin (manager for a complex) by sending an invite email. The Admin sets their password and gets access to their complex management portal.

5. Apartment Admin Setup Journey

  • Configure apartment units and owner/tenant allocations.
  • Add building staff: receptionists, guards, technicians.
  • Define visitor entry rules, maintenance schedules, escalation rules.

6. Staff Operations

  • Receptionists manage visitor entries and exits, QR badge generation.
  • Guards manage physical entry points and visitor approvals.
  • Technicians handle maintenance complaints and preventive maintenance tasks.

7. Tenant Interactions

  • Tenants register their visitors ahead of time.
  • Raise complaints (maintenance, service quality, safety issues).
  • Track maintenance status and provide feedback on services.

8. Visitor Management

  • Visitors either pre-register or are manually registered on arrival.
  • QR codes generated for visitors to speed up check-in and check-out.
  • Hosts receive real-time notifications about their visitor's arrival.

9. Complaint Handling and Escalations

  • Complaint tickets are automatically escalated if not resolved within the defined SLA.
  • Admins monitor ticket aging and take proactive steps for resolution.

10. Reporting and Insights

  • Super Admins and Apartment Admins can view reports on visitor volume, complaint resolution rates, maintenance tasks, staff performance, tenant satisfaction scores.
  • Reports can be downloaded monthly for audits and improvements.
