Apartment Management SaaS - Overview

UpBuild is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for efficient management of apartments, visitors, maintenance, staff, and complaints, ensuring smooth operation and communication between admins, staff, and tenants.

User Flow Story (Detailed)

1. Super Admin Setup

  • Logs in securely to the UpBuild platform.
  • Creates new apartment complexes: Name, Address, Units, Contact Info.
  • Defines global rules for maintenance, visitors, complaints.

2. Assigning Apartment Admin

  • Assigns an Apartment Admin (Manager) per complex.
  • Sends an invite email with login credentials.

3. Apartment Admin Management

  • Adds Receptionists, Guards, Technicians as staff.
  • Defines units and tenants inside the complex.
  • Sets visitor entry rules, complaint escalation timelines, and staff shift rules.

4. Staff Operations

  • Receptionists manage visitor check-in/out and badge issuance.
  • Guards monitor entry gates and security.
  • Technicians resolve maintenance complaints and update statuses.

5. Tenant Interactions

  • Log in through web/mobile apps.
  • Pre-register visitors, raise complaints, view maintenance status.
  • Submit housekeeping feedback.

6. Visitor Management

  • Visitors pre-register or register manually at gates.
  • QR code badges generated.
  • Real-time notifications to tenants.

8. Maintenance Flow

  • Tenants submit tickets (with optional images/videos).

9. Performance Reports

  • Visitor logs, complaint closure rates, staff attendance, and maintenance costs tracked.
  • Monthly exportable reports available for admins.

10. Platform Expansion

  • Super Admin can add more complexes anytime.
  • Future option: Complex-specific Free/Paid plans for scaling.
